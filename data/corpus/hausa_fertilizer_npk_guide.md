 
## Menene NPK
NPK shi ne takin da ke dauke da Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), da Potassium (K) — abubuwa uku da kowane amfanin gona yake bukata mafi yawa. Lambobin akan jaka (misali "15-15-15") suna nuna kashi (%) na kowane bangare.

## Aikin Kowace Bangare
- **Nitrogen (N):** yana taimaka wa ganye da kara girma.
- **Phosphorus (P):** yana taimaka wa saiwa da fure da kwaya.
- **Potassium (K):** yana karfafa lafiyar tsiro da juriya ga cututtuka.

## Yadda Ake Amfani
- A bayar da NPK lokacin shuka (basal), domin Phosphorus da Potassium suna tafiya sannu a kasa kuma suna bukatar kusanci da saiwa tun farko.
- **Ka tabbata an binne takin a karkashin kasa** — kar a bar shi a saman kasa, domin zai yi asara kuma zai iya konawa ganyaye.
- Gwajin kasa (soil test), inda akwai, yana bada shawara mafi dacewa fiye da adadin gama-gari.

*Tushen bayani: Federal Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development jagorar amfani da taki a Najeriya.*
