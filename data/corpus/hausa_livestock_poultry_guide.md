 
## Babbar Cuta
**Newcastle Disease** ita ce cuta mafi hatsari ga kaji a Najeriya — za ta iya kashe dukkan garken kaji cikin kwanaki idan ba a yi rigakafi ba.

## Lokacin Rigakafi (a tabbatar da likitan dabbobi)
- Kwana 5-7: rigakafin Newcastle + Bronchitis.
- Kwana 14: rigakafin Gumboro.
- Kwana 21: rigakafin Newcastle na biyu.

## Adana Magani
A adana rigakafi a wuri mai sanyi (firji), kar a bar shi a rana.

## Tsabta
Footbath (wurin tsaftace kafa) a kofar gidan kaji abu ne mai araha amma yana da tasiri wajen hana cuta shiga.
