# Jagorar Noman Rogo (Hausa Cassava Guide)

> **MUHIMMIN BAYANI GA KUNGIYAR:** AI ne ya rubuta wannan takarda, fassara ce ta `cassava_production.md`. **Bukatar dubawa daga mai magana da Hausa.**

## Gabatarwa
Najeriya ita ce kasa mafi noman rogo a duniya. Rogo yana bayar da rabin abincin yau da kullum ga mutane da yawa a Najeriya.

## Shuka
- Ana shuka rogo ta hanyar yankakken sandar tsiro (stem cuttings), ba iri ba.
- A girka kasa sosai (harrowing) kafin shuka domin saiwar rogo ta girma sosai.
- A sharar ciyawa sau 3 ko fiye, domin ciyawa tana hana rogo girma da kyau.
- Hada rogo da legumes (gyada, wake, soya) yana kara kyawun kasa.

## Babbar Cuta: Cassava Mosaic
Cutar Mosaic na iya lalata kashi 70% na girbi. **Mafi muhimmin maganinta shi ne yin amfani da iri masu juriya da tsaftataccen sandar shuka** — kar a dauki sandar shuka daga gonar da take da alamun cuta (ganye mai tabo-tabo).

## Girbi
- A girbe rogo ne kawai idan akwai kasuwa ko shirin sarrafa shi, domin yana lalacewa da sauri bayan girbi.
- A sarrafa shi cikin awa 48 bayan girbi domin samun ingancin da ya kamata.
