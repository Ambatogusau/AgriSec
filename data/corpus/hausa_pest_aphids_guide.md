 
## Gano Kwarin
Aphids kanana ne, masu launin ja-toka, suna cin sabuwar ganye da tsire-tsire kanana. Suna shafar gyada, wake, da sauran amfanin gona.

## Me Yasa Yake Hatsari
Aphids ba kawai suna cin ganye ba — suna kuma dauke da cutar rosette zuwa gyada, wadda za ta iya lalata gonar duka idan ta yadu.

## Yadda Ake Rage Shi
- Shuka gyada da wuri kuma kusa-kusa (closer spacing) yana rage cutar rosette.
- Yi amfani da irin gyada masu juriya (kamar Samnut 22).
- Bari kwari masu amfani kamar ladybirds su yi aiki — kar a yi amfani da maganin kwari mai karfi wanda zai kashe su ma.
- Maganin sinadarai kawai idan matsalar ta yi tsanani.
