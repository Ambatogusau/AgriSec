## Iri Biyu na Shinkafa  
Akwai shinkafar fadama (lowland), wadda ake nomawa a wuraren da ruwa yake tarawa, da shinkafar tudu (upland), wadda ake nomawa a kasa mai busawa.

## Shuka  
- **Shinkafar fadama (dasawa):** a dasa tsire-tsire 2-3 a kowane rami, zurfin 3-4 cm, nisa 20x20 cm zuwa 30x30 cm dangane da yawan taki da kasa.
- **Shinkafar tudu:** a shuka iri 5-6 a kowane rami, nisa 20x20 ko 30x30 cm, sannan a rage zuwa tsire-tsire 3-4 makonni 2-3 bayan shuka.
- A shuka lokacin da ruwan sama ya tabbata sosai, galibi a watan Mayu/Yuni.

## Taki  
- **Shinkafar fadama:** jaka 4 na NPK 15-15-15 a kowace hekta a lokacin shuka/dasawa, sannan jaka 2-3 na Urea makonni 6-8 bayan shuka (ana iya rabu zuwa sau biyu domin rage asarar Nitrogen a kasa mai ruwa).
- **Shinkafar tudu:** kama da na fadama amma a binne takin NPK sosai a karkashin kasa domin rage asara.
- **Kada a yi taki lokacin da ganyayen suna jika ko kafin ruwan sama mai karfi** — takin zai bata ba zai amfana shuka ba.

## Cire Ciyawa da Cututtuka (Weeding and Disease)
- A yi sharar ciyawa makonni 2-3 da kuma makonni 5-6 bayan shuka/dasawa.
- **Striga** (ciyawar mayya) na iya cutar da shinkafa; ana rage ta ta hanyar amfani da iri marasa Striga, jujjuya noma tare da legumes, da kuma sharewa kafin Striga ta yi iri.
- A lura da alamun "dead-heart" daga stem borer a farkon girma.

## Bayan Girbi  
- A bar tsawon kara (15-20 cm) domin sauki wajen sussukawa da hannu.
- A shanya hatsi a hankali na kwana 2-3 domin rage karyewar hatsi; **kada a shanya a kasa kai tsaye ko a gefen titi**, domin gurbatawa.
- A ajiye a wuri mai sanyi, busasshe, kuma marar beraye.
