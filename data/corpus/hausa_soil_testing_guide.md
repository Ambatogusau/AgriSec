 

## Me Yasa Ake Bukatar Gwajin Kasa
Adadin taki da aka ba a wannan littafin (misali "jaka 6 na NPK a kowace hekta") shawara ce ta gama-gari, ba takamaimai ga kowace gona ba. Kasashe daban-daban suna bukatar taki daban-daban.

## Yadda Ake Gane Bukatar Kasa
1. **Duban alamomi a tsiro** (rawaya, karancin girma) — mafi sauki amma ba daidai ba koyaushe.
2. **Gwajin ganye** — ya fi daidai amma sai bayan an shuka.
3. **Gwajin kasa kafin shuka** — mafi daidai, domin yana bada shawarar taki KAFIN lokacin noma ya fara.

## Ina Za A Samu Gwajin Kasa
- Ofishin noma na jiha (ADP).
- Jami'o'i da cibiyoyin bincike (kamar IITA).
- Kamfanonin gwajin kasa masu zaman kansu (akwai wani a Ibadan).
