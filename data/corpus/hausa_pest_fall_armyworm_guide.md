 

## Gano Kwarin
Tsutsar Fall Armyworm tana cin ganyen masara, ana ganin ramuka kamar "taga" akan ganyaye, da kuma kashin tsutsa (frass) kusa da tsakiyar tsiro.

## Yadda Ake Rage Shi
- Shuka da wuri tare da farkon ruwan sama yana rage matsalar.
- Hada noman masara da legumes (kamar wake) yana taimakawa.
- Ka duba gonar sau biyu a mako a farkon makonni 6 bayan shuka.

## Maganin Arha
- Cire kwai da tsutsa kanana da hannu, a murkushe su.
- A zuba toka ko yashi a tsakiyar tsiro domin hana tsutsa.
- Man neem (botanical extract) zai iya maye gurbin sinadarai masu tsada, a zuba shi da yamma.

## Lokacin Neman Taimako
Idan kashi 20% na tsiro suna da lalacewa a tsakiya, ka tuntubi jami'in noma (extension officer).
