 

## Albasa
- Ana noma albasa da ban ruwa a lokacin rani a jihohin Kano, Sokoto, Jigawa, Katsina, da sauransu.
- **Albasa tana shan wahala da rashin ruwa kafin ka gan ta**, don haka a yi ban ruwa akai-akai: sau biyu a wata Nuwamba-Fabrairu, sannan kowane mako Maris-Mayu.
- **A rage ban ruwa lokacin da albasar take girma** (bayan makonni 8) — yawan ruwa zai sa ta yi laushi kuma ta rube cikin sauki wajen ajiya.

## Tattasai
- Taki NPK 15-15-15 a zuba makonni 2 bayan dasawa, a wani gefen tsiro, ba kusa da gindi ba.
- Babban kwarin tattasai shi ne kwarin 'ya'yan itace (fruit fly) — tsabtace gona daga 'ya'yan da suka fadi yana taimakawa.
