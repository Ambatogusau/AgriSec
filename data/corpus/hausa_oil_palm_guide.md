 
## Gabatarwa
Dabino mai (oil palm) jari ne na dogon lokaci — itace daya na iya bayar da 'ya'ya har shekaru 25 bayan ya girma. Najeriya tana cikin manyan kasashen duniya wajen noman dabino mai.

## Irin Tsiro
- **Dura:** irin gargajiya, yana daukan shekara 6-8 kafin ya fara bayar da 'ya'ya, amma yana da juriya ga wasu kwari.
- **Tenera:** irin hadaka (hybrid) mafi shahara a Najeriya, yana bayar da mai mai yawa kuma yana da juriya ga cututtuka.

## Shuka
- A shuka da nisa kimanin mita 9 tsakanin kowane tsiro (triangular pattern). Wannan zai bada tsiro kimanin 145 a kowace hekta.
- A sa taki mai phosphorus (kimanin gram 400-450) a cikin kowane rami kafin dasa tsiro.

## Taki (Bayan Girma)
- Dabino mai yana bukatar taki sosai — NPK da Magnesium, kashi-kashi (split) sau hudu a shekara, daga watan Yuni/Yuli.
- A zuba takin da nisa (50-75 cm) daga gindin itace, kar a zuba kusa da shi domin kar ya kone shi.

## Kwari da Cututtuka
Babban barazana sun hada da beraye, kwari masu cin ganye, da rhinoceros beetle. A kiyaye gonar daga itatuwan da suka lalace (domin kar su jawo kwari), kuma a kiyaye macizai da sauran dabbobin da suke kashe beraye maimakon a kashe su.
