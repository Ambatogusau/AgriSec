 

## Menene Takin Dabbobi
Takin dabbobi (kashin shanu, akuya, tumaki, da sauransu) da kuma ragowar amfanin gona da suka rube, suna kara kyawun kasa a hankali, ba kamar NPK ko Urea da suke aiki da sauri ba.

## Amfanin Sa
- Yana kara kyawun kasa, musamman a kasashen yashi kamar wadanda ake noman gyada da gero a Arewa.
- Yana rage bukatar sayen taki na kasuwa.
- Hada Urea da takin dabbobi (misali kashi 75% Urea da kashi 25% taki dabba) na iya bada sakamako mafi kyau fiye da kowanne shi kadai.

## Yadda Ake Amfani
- A zuba takin dabbobi kafin shuka, a hada shi da kasa lokacin shirya gona.
- **Ga gyada musamman:** kar a zuba takin dabbobi lokacin girma (a tsakiyar lokacin noma) — yana iya jawo kwarin "white grub". A zuba shi kafin shuka kawai.

*Tushen bayani: Federal Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development; jagororin noman shinkafa da gyada.*
