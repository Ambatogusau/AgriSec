# Jagorar Taki Urea (Hausa Urea Fertilizer Guide)

> **MUHIMMIN BAYANI GA KUNGIYAR:** AI ne ya rubuta wannan takarda, fassara ce ta bayanin Ingilishi a `fertilizer_urea.md`. **Bukatar dubawa daga mai magana da Hausa.**

## Menene Urea
Urea shi ne takin Nitrogen mafi karfi (kimanin 46% Nitrogen), kuma shi ne mai araha fiye da sauran takin Nitrogen.

## Yadda Ake Amfani
- Ana amfani da Urea galibi a matsayin "top-dressing" — wato a zuba shi lokacin da tsiro yana girma, bayan an riga an zuba NPK lokacin shuka.
- **Kada a zuba Urea a kan ganyayen da suke jika** — zai manne ya kona ganyaye.
- **Kada a zuba Urea kafin ruwan sama mai karfi** — zai wanke shi kafin tsiro ya amfana, ka kuma yi asarar kudi.
- A binne Urea a karkashin kasa maimakon barin shi a sama, domin kar ya tashi sama kamar iska (volatilization) ya yi asara.

## Misalan Lokaci
- **Masara:** Urea na biyu makonni 4-5 bayan shuka.
- **Shinkafar fadama:** a rabu Urea sau biyu — makonni 3-4 da makonni 6-8 bayan shuka.

*Tushen bayani: Afrimash jagorar amfani da Urea; jagororin noma na Najeriya.*
