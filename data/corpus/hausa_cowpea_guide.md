 

## Gabatarwa
Wake amfanin gona ne mai muhimmanci ga abinci da kudin shiga a Arewacin Najeriya, kuma yana kara kyawun kasa ga sauran amfanin gona.

## Kwari
Babban kwarin wake shi ne wanda yake cin furanni da kwaya (pod borer), sannan akwai aphids da kwarin ajiya (weevils) bayan girbi.

## Yadda Ake Aiki
- Hada wake da masara, gero, ko dawa yana kara amfanin kasa.
- **Ajiyar wake bayan girbi:** a yi amfani da buhu mai rufe sosai (hermetic/PICS bags) domin hana kwarin ajiya su lalata wake ba tare da sinadarai ba.
