## Gabatarwa  
Masara na daga cikin hatsi mafiya yaduwa a Nijeriya. Ana iya shukarta a yankuna daban-daban, daga gandun daji zuwa savannar Sudan, domin ba ta da tasiri sosai ga tsawon rana.

## Shuka  
- A shuka da zaran an gama shirya gona, domin ciyawa ba za ta riga shukar girma ba.
- Nisa tsakanin layuka: kimanin 75 cm; nisa tsakanin tsire-tsire: 50 cm, da tsire-tsire 2 a kowane rami.
- Ana bukatar iri kimanin kilo 15-20 a kowace hekta. A shuka iri 3 a kowane rami, sannan a rage zuwa tsire-tsire 2 makonni 1-2 bayan tsiro.
- A yi amfani da iri ingantacce (certified seed) na nau'in da ya dace da yankin.

## Taki  
- Shawarar gama-gari a savannar Nijeriya: kimanin kilo 100 na Nitrogen (N), kilo 50 na Phosphorus (P2O5), da kilo 50 na Potassium (K2O) a kowace hekta, a rabu sau biyu.
- **Mataki na farko:** a shuka lokacin dashe ko cikin sati na farko, misali jaka 6 na NPK 15-15-15 a kowace hekta.
- **Mataki na biyu:** a yi amfani da Urea (kimanin jaka 2 a kowace hekta) makonni 4-5 bayan shuka.
- **Ka tabbata an binne takin a karkashin kasa** — kada a bar shi a saman kasa, domin zai yi asara kuma zai iya konawa ganyaye.

## Cire Ciyawa  
A yi sharar ciyawa sau biyu: kusan makonni 2 da makonni 4-5 bayan shuka.

## Manyan Kwari da Cututtuka (Key Pests and Diseases)
- **Fall Armyworm (tsutsa mai cin masara):** mafi hatsari a cikin kwari, duba takardar "pest_fall_armyworm" don karin bayani.
- **Stem borer (tsutsa mai huda kara):** yana sa tsakiyar tsiro ya bushe ("dead-heart").
- **Striga (ciyawar fari/mayya):** ciyawa mai cutar da masara; ana rage ta ta hanyar noma jujjuyawa da legumes (kamar wake/groundnut) da kuma amfani da iri mai juriya.

## Girbi  
A bar masara ta bushe sosai a gona kafin girbi, domin rage barnar da take faruwa wajen ajiya (kamar aflatoxin).
