## Muhimmin Bayani
**Kar a dogara da kalandar kawai** — ruwan sama na farko a Afrilu/Mayu ba koyaushe shi ne ainihin farkon damina ba. Idan an shuka da wuri akan ruwa daya, akwai hadarin tsiro ya bushe idan ruwa bai dawo ba.

A tuntubi ofishin noma na yankin domin sanin lokacin ainihin damina a wannan shekara, domin yana canzawa kowace shekara.
