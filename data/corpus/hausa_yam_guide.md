 
## Gabatarwa
Najeriya ita ce kasa mafi noman doya a duniya. Doya tana bukatar kasa mai laushi, mai zubar ruwa da kyau.

## Shuka
- Ana shuka doya da "setts" — yankakken sashin doyar iri, ba iri na gaske ba.
- Ana shuka a kan tudu (heaps/ridges) domin zubar ruwa da kyau.
- Lokacin noma yana da tsawo — wata 8-12, ya danganta da irin doya.

## Tsayawa (Staking)
**Dole ne a bayar wa doya sanda da za ta hau** — wannan yana kara hasken rana ga ganye, kuma yana kara girbi sosai.

## Hada Noma
Domin doya tana daukan tsawon lokaci, manoma sukan hada ta da masara da rogo, domin samun kudin shiga kafin doya ta girbi.

## Cututtuka
- **Anthracnose:** cuta mafi yawaita, tana sa kara ya bushe.
- **Dry rot:** mafi hatsari a lokacin ajiya — yana lalata doya a ajiye, don haka kyakkyawan shanyawa kafin ajiya yana da muhimmanci.
